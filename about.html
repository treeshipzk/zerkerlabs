About

Zerker Labs is a product lab working on one problem: trust in AI.

Not trust as a brand promise. Trust as something you can verify, audit, and carry with you without calling a vendor to confirm it. As agents do more — making payments, running workflows, delegating to other agents — the question of what actually happened becomes load-bearing.

We think the answer is infrastructure, not dashboards. Portable, verifiable records that travel with the action, owned by whoever ran it, readable by anyone without trusting us.

First Product

treeship.dev — local-first, portable trust receipts for agent actions. Ed25519-signed, chainable across hops, verifiable offline. Open source, self-hostable. treeship.dev
